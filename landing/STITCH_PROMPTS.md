# Praesidia — Google Stitch Prompts (v2)

## Brand Direction
- **Logo style**: Starbucks-siren inspired circular medallion with a mythical Roman guardian woman
- **Colors**: Dark navy (#0d1117), Electric blue (#58a6ff), Emerald green (#3fb950), Warm gold accents
- **Image style**: Photorealistic for page imagery, emblem/medallion for logo
- **Character**: Praesidia is a Roman goddess of protection/defense — armored, regal, vigilant
